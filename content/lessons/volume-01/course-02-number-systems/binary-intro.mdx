## Binary Numbers

Computers use **binary** — a base-2 number system with only two digits: `0` and `1`.

### Why Binary?

Electronic circuits have two states: on (1) and off (0). Binary maps perfectly to this physical reality.

### Counting in Binary

| Decimal | Binary |
|---------|--------|
| 0 | 0 |
| 1 | 1 |
| 2 | 10 |
| 3 | 11 |
| 4 | 100 |
| 5 | 101 |
| 6 | 110 |
| 7 | 111 |

### Binary to Decimal Conversion

```
Binary: 1 0 1 1
Place:  8 4 2 1
Value:  8+0+2+1 = 11
```

Each position represents a power of 2: 2³, 2², 2¹, 2⁰.
