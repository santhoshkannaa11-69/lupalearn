## 1. Project Brief

Build a calculator that can evaluate expressions with +, -, *, /, handle multiple operations, and manage errors gracefully.

## 2. Requirements

1. Support basic operations: +, -, *, /
2. Handle decimal numbers
3. Prevent division by zero
4. Show a history of calculations
5. Keyboard input support
