## 1. Project Brief

Build a grade tracker that manages students, assignments, and calculates final grades.

## 2. Requirements

1. `addStudent(name)` — adds a student
2. `addGrade(studentName, subject, score)` — records a grade
3. `getAverage(studentName)` — calculates average
4. `getLetterGrade(score)` — converts number to letter
5. `getClassAverage(subject)` — class average per subject
6. `getRankings()` — students sorted by average
