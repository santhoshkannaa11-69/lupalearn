## 1. Hook

Boolean algebra has laws, just like regular algebra. The Identity law says: `A AND 1 = A` and `A OR 0 = A`. A variable combined with its identity value stays unchanged.

## 2. Identity Law

| AND Form | OR Form |
|----------|---------|
| A · 1 = A | A + 0 = A |

### Truth Table Verification

```
A · 1 = A:
A | 1 | Result
0 | 1 |   0
1 | 1 |   1

→ Same as A

A + 0 = A:
A | 0 | Result
0 | 0 |   0
1 | 0 |   1

→ Same as A
```
