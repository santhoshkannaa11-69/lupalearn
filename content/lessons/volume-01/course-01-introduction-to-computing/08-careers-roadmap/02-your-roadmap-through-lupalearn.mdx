## Your Roadmap Through LupaLearn

You've completed **Course 1: Introduction to Computing**. Here's how LupaLearn will guide you through the rest of your journey.

### How the Knowledge Graph Works

LupaLearn organizes knowledge as a **graph** — concepts connected to related concepts. Every lesson, quiz, project, and challenge is linked to the concepts it teaches.

```
Variables ←→ Data Types ←→ Operators ←→ Control Flow
   ↓                                              ↓
Functions ←─────────────────────────────────→ Loops
```

When you learn a concept, the system tracks your **confidence**. When confidence is high, the graph unlocks related concepts.

### Three Learning Modes

| Mode | Best For | How It Works |
|------|----------|--------------|
| **🎓 Guided** | Structured learning | Follow the curriculum step by step |
| **🚀 Explorer** | Self-directed learning | Search any topic, learn on demand |
| **🤖 AI** | Personalized learning | Tell your goal, get a custom roadmap |

### Your AI Tutor

The **AI Tutor** (open it from the sidebar) can:
- Explain any concept at your level
- Generate practice quizzes
- Review your code
- Give hints without giving answers
- Recommend what to learn next

### The Playground

The **Playground** is your coding workspace:
- **Editor** — Write code with syntax highlighting
- **Terminal** — Run commands and execute code
- **Preview** — See HTML/CSS results

### Progress System

| Feature | How It Works |
|---------|--------------|
| **XP** | Earn from lessons, quizzes, challenges |
| **Level** | 20 levels from Newbie to Grandmaster |
| **Streak** | Daily learning maintains your streak |
| **Achievements** | Unlock badges for milestones |

### Certifications

After completing **Programming Fundamentals** (Course 7), you can earn a certificate by:

- Completing all 45 lessons
- Passing all quizzes
- Building the capstone project
- Achieving 80%+ average confidence
