## Data vs Information vs Knowledge

These terms are often confused but represent different levels of understanding.

### The DIKW Pyramid

```
        ┌─────────┐
        │ Wisdom  │  ← Applied knowledge
        ├─────────┤
        │Knowledge│  ← Connected information
        ├─────────┤
        │Information│ ← Processed data
        ├─────────┤
        │  Data   │  ← Raw facts
        └─────────┘
```

### Examples

| Level | Example |
|-------|---------|
| **Data** | `25`, `35`, `28` — raw numbers |
| **Information** | `Temperature: 25°C, 35°C, 28°C` — labeled data |
| **Knowledge** | `Summer temperatures average 29°C` — patterns in information |
| **Wisdom** | `Install better cooling for summer` — knowing what to do |

### In Programming

- **Data** is what your program receives
- **Information** is what your program processes
- **Knowledge** is what your program learns over time
- **Wisdom** is what you design your program to do

Every program you write transforms data into information.
