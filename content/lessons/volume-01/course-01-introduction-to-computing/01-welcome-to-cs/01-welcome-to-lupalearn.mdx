## Welcome to Computer Science

You're about to learn one of the most valuable skills of the 21st century. Computer Science isn't just about coding — it's about **problem-solving**, **logical thinking**, and **understanding how the digital world works**.

### Why Learn Computer Science?

- **Career opportunities** — CS jobs are among the highest-paying and fastest-growing
- **Understanding technology** — Every industry now runs on software
- **Building things** — You can create apps, websites, games, and AI systems
- **Problem-solving skills** — CS teaches you to think systematically
