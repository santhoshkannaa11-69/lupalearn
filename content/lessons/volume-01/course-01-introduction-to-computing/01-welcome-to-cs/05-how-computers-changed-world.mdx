## How Computers Changed the World

Computers have transformed every aspect of modern life.

### Communication

From letters to instant messaging. Email, social media, and video calls connect billions of people globally.

### Healthcare

Computers power MRI and CT scans, maintain electronic health records, and assist in surgery through robotic systems. AI helps detect diseases earlier than human doctors alone.

### Finance

Banking, stock trading, and payments are entirely digital. Algorithms process millions of transactions per second.

### Transportation

GPS navigation, traffic optimization, and autonomous vehicles all rely on computing. Airlines use complex systems to manage thousands of flights daily.

### Space Exploration

Rockets, satellites, and rovers are controlled by computers. The Mars rover Perseverance operates semi-autonomously using onboard AI.

### Artificial Intelligence

AI systems now:
- Recognize faces and voices
- Translate languages
- Create art and music
- Play chess and Go at champion level
- Assist in scientific research
