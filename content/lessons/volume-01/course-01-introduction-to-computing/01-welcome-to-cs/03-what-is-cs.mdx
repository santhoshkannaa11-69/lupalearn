## What Is Computer Science?

**Computer Science** is the study of computation, information processing, and the design of computer systems.

### It's Not Just Programming

Many people think CS = coding, but the field is much broader:

| Branch | What It Studies | Example |
|--------|----------------|---------|
| **Theory** | What can be computed? | Algorithms, complexity |
| **Systems** | How to build reliable software? | Operating systems, databases |
| **AI** | How to make computers intelligent? | Machine learning, robotics |
| **Networks** | How do computers communicate? | Internet, protocols |
| **Security** | How to protect data? | Cryptography, cybersecurity |
| **Graphics** | How to render images? | Video games, CGI |
| **HCI** | How do humans interact with computers? | UI/UX design |

### Real-World Applications

| Field | CS Contribution |
|-------|----------------|
| **Medicine** | MRI imaging, drug discovery, patient records |
| **Finance** | Algorithmic trading, fraud detection |
| **Transportation** | GPS, self-driving cars, traffic optimization |
| **Entertainment** | Streaming, video games, CGI movies |
| **Science** | Weather prediction, genome sequencing |
| **Communication** | Email, social media, video calls |

### Why This Matters

Understanding the breadth of CS helps you choose your path. Every lesson in this school connects to these branches.
