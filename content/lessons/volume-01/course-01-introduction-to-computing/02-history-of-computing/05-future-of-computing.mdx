## The Future of Computing

Computing continues to evolve. Here are the major trends shaping the future.

### Quantum Computing

Unlike classical computers that use bits (0 or 1), quantum computers use **qubits** that can be both 0 and 1 simultaneously (**superposition**).

- Could solve problems impossible for classical computers
- Drug discovery, cryptography, climate modeling
- Companies: IBM, Google, Microsoft, D-Wave

### Artificial Intelligence

AI is moving from pattern recognition to general intelligence.

- **Generative AI** — creates text, images, music, code
- **Autonomous systems** — self-driving cars, drones
- **AI assistants** — proactive, personalized help

### Edge Computing

Instead of sending all data to the cloud, processing happens on devices themselves.

- Faster response times
- Better privacy
- Works offline
- Powers IoT devices

### Neuromorphic Computing

Chips designed to mimic the brain's neural structure — potentially millions of times more energy-efficient than current processors.

### The Trend

```
1940s: One computer per country
1970s: One computer per company
1990s: One computer per person
2020s: Many computers per person (phone, watch, laptop, car)
Future: Computing everywhere
```

The only constant in computing is that it keeps accelerating.
