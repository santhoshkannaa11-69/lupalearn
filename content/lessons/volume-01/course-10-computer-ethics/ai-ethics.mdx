## AI Ethics

As AI becomes more powerful, ethical considerations become critical.

### Key Ethical Concerns

| Concern | Description | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Bias** | AI inherits human prejudices | Facial recognition failing on darker skin |
| **Privacy** | AI often requires大量data | Surveillance, data collection |
| **Accountability** | Who is responsible for AI decisions? | Self-driving car accidents |
| **Job displacement** | AI may replace human workers | Automation of routine jobs |
| **Transparency** | Black-box decision making | Loan denial without explanation |

### Responsible AI Principles

1. **Fairness** — AI should treat all groups equitably
2. **Accountability** — There should be human oversight
3. **Transparency** — Decisions should be explainable
4. **Privacy** — Data collection should be minimized
5. **Safety** — AI systems should be robust and secure

### Your Responsibility

As a developer, you have the power to build AI that helps humanity. Use that power wisely.
