## Bits & Bytes

### The Bit

A **bit** is the smallest unit of data — a single 0 or 1.

### The Byte

A **byte** is 8 bits. It can represent 256 values (2⁸).

### Storage Units

| Unit | Size | Rough Equivalent |
|------|------|------------------|
| 1 byte | 8 bits | One character |
| 1 KB | 1,024 bytes | A short paragraph |
| 1 MB | 1,024 KB | A photograph |
| 1 GB | 1,024 MB | A movie |
| 1 TB | 1,024 GB | A hard drive |

### Endianness

- **Big-endian**: Most significant byte stored first
- **Little-endian**: Least significant byte stored first

Most modern computers use **little-endian** for performance reasons.
