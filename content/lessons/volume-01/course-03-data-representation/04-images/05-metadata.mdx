## 1. Hook

Every photo you take with your phone contains hidden data: the exact time, GPS coordinates, camera model, aperture, shutter speed, and even the lens used. That's EXIF metadata.

## 2. What's in EXIF

| Field | What It Stores |
|-------|---------------|
| **Date/time** | When the photo was taken |
| **GPS** | Where it was taken |
| **Camera** | Make, model, lens |
| **Settings** | Aperture, shutter speed, ISO |
| **Thumbnail** | A small preview image |

## 3. Privacy Implications

GPS metadata means photos shared online can reveal your home address, daily routine, and location history. Many social media platforms strip EXIF data automatically — but not all.

## 4. Summary

- **EXIF** data stores camera settings, location, and timing
- Metadata reveals privacy-sensitive information
- Always check what metadata you're sharing
